Joshemve is a Minecraft content creator focused on modded gameplay, modpack events, and community-driven series. From massive war simulations to deep magic progression, every pack is built to create a unique experience for players and viewers alike.

This archive is the home of a few of the modpacks Joshemve has released — past and present. Each pack (if there is one) is tied to a YouTube video.

We are currently working on having more modpacks from older videos. If you have any older modpack or would like to contribute or make a suggestion, feel free to contact our team at: joshemve-modpack-archive@proton.me
